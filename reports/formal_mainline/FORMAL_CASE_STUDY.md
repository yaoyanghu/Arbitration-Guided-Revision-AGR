## Readout

- temporal successes show that the model can reject retrospective year mentions and choose query-relevant years
- conflict successes show that the model can prefer fresher same-title evidence when answer-bearing values disagree
- failures show that the remaining ceiling is no longer simple retrieval, but answer/evidence discrimination and relation-sensitive temporal reasoning
